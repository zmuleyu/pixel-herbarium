## Description (4000 chars)

```
See a flower? Point your camera.

Pixel Herbarium turns your real-world flower discoveries into charming pixel art. AI identifies the plant, then transforms your photo into a one-of-a-kind pixel illustration for your personal herbarium.

Every flower comes with its hanakotoba — the Japanese language of flowers — adding a layer of meaning to each discovery.


HOW IT WORKS

1. Photograph a flower with your camera
2. AI identifies the species instantly
3. Your photo becomes unique pixel art
4. Discover the flower's hanakotoba (flower language)
5. Add it to your collection


FEATURES

- AI Plant Identification — snap and identify in seconds
- Pixel Art Generation — your photos become art
- Hanakotoba Database — Japanese + Western flower meanings
- Flip Card — tap to reveal the Western meaning on the back
- 120 Species Collection — discover and collect them all
- Seasonal Rhythm — Budding → In Bloom → Fading
- City Map — GPS-tagged discoveries with heat map
- Bouquet Gifting — send pixel art bouquets to friends
- Share Poster — Instagram Stories & LINE Card formats


WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT

Unlike plant identification apps that stop at "what is this plant?", Pixel Herbarium turns identification into the beginning of something creative. Your discovery becomes pixel art. The flower's meaning is revealed through an interactive flip card. Your collection grows with the seasons.

It's part field guide, part art project, part seasonal companion.


SEASONAL DISCOVERY

Spring brings cherry blossoms and tulips. Summer brings sunflowers and morning glories. Each season reveals new flowers to discover, and rare species appear only during narrow windows.

No countdowns. No pressure. Just the gentle rhythm of nature.


FREE TO START

Identify up to 5 plants per month for free. Start with the flowers around you.


PRIVACY

- Discovery locations are fuzzed by ~100m
- Map visibility can be toggled off
- Full data export and account deletion supported
```
