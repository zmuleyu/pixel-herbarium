## Description (4000 chars)

```
Visit Japan's sakura spots. Stamp your photos. Build your flower travel journal.

Hanazukan (花図鉑) is a check-in app for Japan's flower spots. When you visit a famous sakura location, select a photo, choose a stamp style, and create a keepsake photo with a beautiful watermark stamp. Track everywhere you've been, and share your flower moments with friends.


HOW IT WORKS

1. Browse Japan's 100 famous flower spots — cherry blossoms, hydrangeas, sunflowers and more
2. Visit a spot and open the check-in screen
3. Pick a photo from your camera or library
4. Choose your stamp style: Pixel, Seal, or Minimal
5. Save or share your stamped photo on LINE, Instagram, or Twitter


YOUR STAMP STYLES

Three styles to match your mood:

· Pixel — charming dot-art style stamp with spot name and date
· Seal — elegant Japanese hanko-inspired ink stamp
· Minimal — clean, modern label with location and season

Each stamp tells the story of where you were and when.


YOUR FOOTPRINT

Every check-in you make is saved to your Footprint grid. Scroll back through the places you've visited across seasons — spring sakura, summer hydrangeas, autumn leaves. Your flower journey, in one place.


SHARE YOUR MOMENTS

Share stamped photos directly to LINE, Instagram, or Twitter. A photo with a stamp says more than words — it says "I was here."


GUEST MODE — START INSTANTLY

No login required. Open the app and start checking in as a guest. Create an account with Apple Sign In to save your history to the cloud.


WHY HANAZUKAN

· Completely free — check-in, stamps, sharing: all free, always
· No advertisements — just flowers
· No forced sign-up — guest mode works immediately
· Data export and account deletion supported


GREAT FOR

- Sakura season flower viewing (hanami) enthusiasts
- Travelers visiting Japan's famous garden spots
- People who love stamp rallies and collection apps
- Pixel art and retro design fans
- Families and friends exploring nature together


FEATURES

· Flower Spot Check-in — 100 famous spots across Japan
· Stamp Watermark Photos — Pixel / Seal / Minimal styles
· Footprint Grid — your flower journey history
· Photo Sharing — LINE, Instagram, Twitter
· Seasonal Theme — sakura season colors and animations
· Guest Mode — no sign-up needed


PRIVACY

· Location is only used at check-in (no background tracking)
· Full data export and account deletion supported


Sakura season comes once a year.
Start your flower journey with Hanazukan.
```
