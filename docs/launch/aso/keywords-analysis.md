## Keyword Strategy

### Tier 1: Low competition, high relevance (primary targets)
- `ピクセルアート` — zero competition in plant app space
- `花言葉` — cultural keyword, emotional search intent
- `花図鑑` — more specific than `植物図鑑`, less competition

### Tier 2: Medium competition, seasonal spikes
- `桜` — massive seasonal search volume (March-April)
- `季節の花` — year-round relevance
- `散歩` — lifestyle intent, matches target user behavior

### Tier 3: High competition (presence required, not primary)
- `植物 識別` — dominated by PictureThis
- `AI カメラ` — generic, high competition
- `図鑑` — broad, Biome dominates

### Excluded (too competitive / off-brand)
- `ガーデニング` — GreenSnap territory, not PH's focus
- `育て方` — care/cultivation, not PH's scope
- `多肉植物` — niche within GreenSnap's domain

## Category Rationale

**Primary: Lifestyle** (not Education)
- PictureThis + Biome both use Education → saturated
- GreenSnap uses Photo & Video → different positioning
- Lifestyle aligns with PH's "seasonal companion" identity
- Lower competition for plant-adjacent apps in Lifestyle

**Secondary: Entertainment**
- Reinforces the gamification/collection/art angle
- Differentiates from utility-focused competitors
